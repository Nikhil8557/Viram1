The Philosophy

Stopping Time
& Saving Lives

Viruses mutate faster than we can analyze them. Viram bridges this gap by using deep learning to predict where a virus is going, not just where it has been.

Why "Viram"?

Derived from Sanskrit, Viram (विराम) means "Stop". Our mission is to stop slow vaccine development and stop pandemic outbreaks before they start.

Trajectory Mapping

Maps evolutionary pathways to predict next-gen mutations.

Weak Variant Detection

Identifies stable but pathologically weak variants for vaccines.

Pandemic Readiness

Detects transmission resistance potential weeks in advance.
